                                 A BILL


 
To provide that no pay adjustment for Members of Congress shall be made 
    with respect to any pay period occurring during the One Hundred 
                          Fourteenth Congress.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NO PAY ADJUSTMENT FOR MEMBERS OF CONGRESS DURING ONE HUNDRED 
              FOURTEENTH CONGRESS.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no adjustment shall be 
made under section 601(a) of the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946 
(2 U.S.C. 4501), relating to compensation of Members of Congress, with 
respect to any pay period occurring during the One Hundred Fourteenth 
Congress.
