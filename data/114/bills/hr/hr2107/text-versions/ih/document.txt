                                 A BILL


 
To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to clarify the treatment 
                of certain Medicaid enrollment brokers.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TREATMENT OF CERTAIN MEDICAID BROKERS.

    Section 1903(b)(4) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 
1396b(b)(4)) is amended--
            (1) in the matter before subparagraph (A), by inserting 
        after ``respect to the broker'' the following: ``(or, in the 
        case of subparagraph (A) or subparagraph (B)(i), if the 
        Secretary finds that the broker has established and maintains 
        policies and procedures to ensure the independence of its 
        enrollment activities from the interests of any managed care 
        entity or provider)''; and
            (2) in subparagraph (B)--
                    (A) by inserting ``(i)'' after ``either''; and
                    (B) by inserting ``(ii)'' after ``health care 
                provider or''.
