                                 A BILL


 
To limit the amount authorized to be appropriated to carry out chapter 
   2 of title IV of the Immigration and Nationality Act, relating to 
                         refugee resettlement.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.

    Section 414(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 
1524(a)) is amended to read as follows:
    ``(a) There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this 
chapter $1,192,000,000 for fiscal year 2017 and each subsequent fiscal 
year.''.
