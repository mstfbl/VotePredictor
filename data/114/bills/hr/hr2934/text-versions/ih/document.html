                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend the authority of the 
        Advisory Committee on Homeless Veterans for five years.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FIVE-YEAR EXTENSION OF ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON HOMELESS 
              VETERANS.

    Section 2066(d) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``December 31, 2015'' and inserting ``December 31, 2020''.
