                                 An Act


 
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 
 3130 Grants Lake Boulevard in Sugar Land, Texas, as the ``LCpl Garrett 
                 W. Gamble, USMC Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. LCPL GARRETT W. GAMBLE, USMC POST OFFICE BUILDING.
    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 3130 Grants Lake Boulevard in Sugar Land, Texas, shall be 
known and designated as the ``LCpl Garrett W. Gamble, USMC Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``LCpl 
Garrett W. Gamble, USMC Post Office Building''.
