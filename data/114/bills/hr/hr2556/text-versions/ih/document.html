                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Federal Water Pollution Control Act to repeal the 
  authorization for program development and implementation grants for 
 coastal recreation water quality monitoring and notification, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF PROGRAM DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION GRANTS 
              AUTHORIZATION.

    Section 406(b) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 
U.S.C. 1346(b)) is amended by adding at the end the following new 
paragraph:
            ``(5) Repeal of authority.--The Administrator may not make 
        a grant to a State or local government under this subsection 
        after the date of enactment of this paragraph.''.
