                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act by repealing 
                  the paid lunch equity requirements.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``School Lunch Affordability Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF PAID EQUITY REQUIREMENTS.

    Subsection (p) of section 12 of the Richard B. Russell National 
School Lunch Act (42 U.S.C. 1760) is repealed.
