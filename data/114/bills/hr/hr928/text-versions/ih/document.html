                                 A BILL


 
 To repeal the annual fee on health insurance providers enacted by the 
              Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF ANNUAL FEE ON HEALTH INSURANCE PROVIDERS.

    The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act is amended by 
striking section 9010.
