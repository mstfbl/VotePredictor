                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for the compensation of furloughed Department of Homeland 
 Security employees in the event of a lapse in Department of Homeland 
            Security appropriations, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``DHS Employee Retroactive Pay 
Fairness Act''.

SEC. 2. COMPENSATION FOR FURLOUGHED FEDERAL EMPLOYEES DUE TO LAPSE IN 
              DEPARTMENT OF HOMELAND SECURITY FUNDING.

    Any Federal employee who is furloughed as a result of any lapse in 
appropriations which begins on or about February 27, 2015, shall be 
compensated at such employee's standard rate of compensation, for the 
period of such lapse, as soon as practicable after such lapse in 
appropriations ends.
