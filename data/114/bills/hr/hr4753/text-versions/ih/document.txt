                                 A BILL


 
To exclude from consideration as income under the United States Housing 
 Act of 1937 certain veterans compensation and pensions, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Wounded Warrior Housing Reform Act 
of 2016''.

SEC. 2. EXCLUSIONS FROM INCOME.

    Paragraph (4) of section 3(b) of the United States Housing Act of 
1937 (42 U.S.C. 1437a(b)(4)) is amended--
            (1) by inserting ``(A)'' after ``except that'';
            (2) by striking ``and any amounts'' and inserting ``, (B) 
        any amounts'';
            (3) by striking ``or any deferred'' and inserting ``, (C) 
        any deferred''; and
            (4) by inserting after ``prospective monthly amounts'' the 
        following: ``, and (D) in the case only of a veteran (as such 
        term is defined in section 101 of title 38, United States 
        Code), any disability compensation under chapter 11 of title 
        38, United States Code, and amounts from a pension paid under 
        section 1513 of title 38, United States Code,''.
