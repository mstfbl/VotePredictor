                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend the authority to 
  provide work-study allowance for certain activities by individuals 
 receiving educational assistance by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Work-Study for Student Veterans 
Act''.

SEC. 2. WORK-STUDY ALLOWANCE.

    Section 3485(a)(4) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``June 30, 2013'' each place it appears and inserting ``June 
30, 2020''.
