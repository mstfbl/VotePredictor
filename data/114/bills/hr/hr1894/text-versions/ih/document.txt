                                 A BILL


 
To authorize the extension of preferential tariff treatment for certain 
                  textile goods imported from Morocco.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF TARIFF PREFERENCE LEVEL PROGRAM FOR MOROCCO.

    (a) In General.--U.S. Note 64(b) to subchapter XII of chapter 99 of 
the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended--
            (1) by striking ``shall be as follows:'' and all that 
        follows through ``As used in this note'' and inserting ``shall 
        be 10,000,000 SME for each of the calendar years 2016 through 
        2025. As used in this note''; and
            (2) by striking ``December 31, 2015'' and inserting 
        ``December 31, 2025''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendments made by this section shall 
apply with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for 
consumption, on or after January 1, 2016.
