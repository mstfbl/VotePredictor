                                 A BILL


 
  To amend title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972 to define the 
                term ``sex'' for purposes of such title.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Title IX Clarification Act of 
2016''.

SEC. 2. AMENDMENTS.

    Section 901(c) of the Education Amendments of 1972 (20 U.S.C. 
1681(c)) is amended--
            (1) by striking ``title an educational institution'' and 
        inserting the following:
    ``title--
            ``(1) the term `educational institution' means'',
            (2) by striking the period at the end and inserting ``; 
        and'', and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
            ``(2) the term `sex' means with respect to an individual 
        the biological sex of such individual.''.
