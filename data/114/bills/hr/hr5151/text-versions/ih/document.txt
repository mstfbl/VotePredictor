                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 10, United States Code, to provide an extension of the 
special survivor indemnity allowance provided to widows and widowers of 
          certain deceased members of the uniformed services.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF SPECIAL SURVIVOR INDEMNITY ALLOWANCE.

    (a) Payment Amount Per Fiscal Year.--Paragraph (2)(I) of section 
1450(m) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking 
``fiscal year 2017'' and inserting ``fiscal years 2017 and 2018''.
    (b) Duration.--Paragraph (6) of such section is amended--
            (1) by striking ``September 30, 2017'' and inserting 
        ``September 30, 2018''; and
            (2) by striking ``October 1, 2017'' each place it appears 
        and inserting ``October 1, 2018''.
