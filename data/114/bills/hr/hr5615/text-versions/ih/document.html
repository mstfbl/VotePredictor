                                 A BILL


 
         To reinstate the ban on semiautomatic assault weapons.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Freedom From Fear Act''.

SEC. 2. REINSTATEMENT OF BAN ON SEMIAUTOMATIC ASSAULT WEAPONS.

    Notwithstanding section 110105 of the Violent Crime Control and Law 
Enforcement Act of 1994 (Public Law 103-322), sections 110102 and 
110106 of such Act are hereby re-enacted into law, except that, in lieu 
of the matter to be inserted by the amendment made by section 
110102(c)(1) of such Act, the matter to be inserted shall be ``(q), or 
(v) of section 922''.
