                                 A BILL


 
  To repeal titles I and II of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and 
                        Consumer Protection Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL.

    Titles I and II of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer 
Protection Act (12 U.S.C. 5301 et seq.) are hereby repealed, and the 
provisions of law amended or repealed by such titles are restored or 
revived as if such titles had not been enacted.
