                                 A BILL


 
  To increase the number of months of vocational educational training 
 that may be counted as work under the temporary assistance for needy 
                           families program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Skills First Act of 2015''.

SEC. 2. INCREASE IN NUMBER OF MONTHS OF VOCATIONAL EDUCATIONAL TRAINING 
              THAT MAY BE COUNTED AS WORK.

    (a) Amendment.--Section 407(d)(8) of the Social Security Act (42 
U.S.C. 607(d)(8)) is amended by striking ``12'' and inserting ``36''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section shall take 
effect on the first day of the first calendar month that begins after 
the date that is 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
