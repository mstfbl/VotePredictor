                                 A BILL


 
To remove a restriction that prohibits the use of Federal funds to pay 
   for maintenance of the memorial to honor Tomas G. Masaryk in the 
                         District of Columbia.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. RESTRICTION ON USE OF FEDERAL FUNDS REMOVED.

    Section 1(c) of Public Law 107-61 is amended by striking ``or its 
maintenance''.
