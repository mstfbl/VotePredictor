                                 A BILL


 
To ensure that the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority complies 
                        with auditing standards.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUDITING STANDARDS REQUIREMENT.

    No Federal funds may be made available to the Metropolitan 
Washington Airports Authority unless the Secretary of the Department of 
Transportation certifies that the Metropolitan Washington Airports 
Authority is complying with appropriate auditing standards.
