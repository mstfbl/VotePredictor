                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the National Endowment for the Arts to use funds to make 
        grants for Literature Fellowships: Translation Projects.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON THE USE OF FUNDS BY THE NATIONAL ENDOWMENT 
              FOR THE ARTS FOR GRANTS FOR LITERATURE FELLOWSHIPS: 
              TRANSLATION PROJECTS.

    No funds available to the National Endowment for the Arts may be 
used to make grants for Literature Fellowships: Translation Projects.
