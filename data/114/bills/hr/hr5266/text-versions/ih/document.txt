                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 10, United States Code, to ensure that information 
regarding the deduction of amounts of disability compensation by reason 
of voluntary separation pay is provided to members of the Armed Forces 
                   separating from the Armed Forces.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Servicemember and Veteran Financial 
Transparency Act''.

SEC. 2. INCLUSION OF INFORMATION IN TRANSITION ASSISTANCE PROGRAM.

    Section 1144(b) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by 
adding at the end the following new paragraph:
            ``(10) Provide information regarding the deduction of 
        disability compensation paid by the Secretary of Veterans 
        Affairs pursuant to section 1175a(h) of this title by reason of 
        voluntary separation pay received by the member.''.
