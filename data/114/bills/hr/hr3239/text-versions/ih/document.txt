                                 A BILL


 
   To provide enhanced security at Armed Forces recruitment centers 
  through the installation of reinforced bullet-proof glass and entry 
                                 doors.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Armed Forces Career Center 
Protection Act''.

SEC. 2. INSTALLATION OF REINFORCED BULLET-PROOF GLASS AND ENTRY DOORS 
              AT ARMED FORCES RECRUITMENT CENTERS.

    The Secretary of each of the military departments, and the 
Secretary of the Department in which the Coast Guard is operating, 
shall provide for the installation of reinforced bullet-proof glass and 
entry doors at Armed Forces recruitment centers, including facilities 
leased for use as recruitment centers, under the jurisdiction of the 
Secretary as an additional security measure to protect members of the 
Armed Forces serving as recruiters and potential recruits and other 
individuals visiting recruitment centers.
