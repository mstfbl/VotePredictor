                                 A BILL


 
To make the current Dungeness crab fishery management regime permanent 
                        and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``West Coast Dungeness Crab Management 
Act''.

SEC. 2. DUNGENESS CRAB FISHERY MANAGEMENT.

    Section 203 of the Act entitled ``An Act to approve a governing 
international agreement between the United States and the Republic of 
Poland, and for other purposes'', approved November 13, 1998 (Public 
Law 105-384; 16 U.S.C. 1856 note) is amended--
            (1) by striking subsection (i); and
            (2) by redesignating subsection (j) as subsection (i).
