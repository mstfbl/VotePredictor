                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Mineral Leasing Act to repeal provisions relating only to 
                     the Allegheny National Forest.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Cooperative Management of Mineral 
Rights Act of 2015''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF PROVISIONS REGARDING THE ALLEGHENY NATIONAL FOREST.

    Subsection (o) of section 17 of the Mineral Leasing Act (30 U.S.C. 
226) and 2508 of the Energy Policy Act of 1992 (Public Law 102-486; 106 
Stat. 3108) are repealed.
