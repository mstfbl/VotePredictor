                                 A BILL


 
To authorize the use of Ebola funds for Zika response and preparedness.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Zika Is Preventable Plan Act'' or 
the ``ZIPP Act''.

SEC. 2. AUTHORIZATION TO USE EBOLA FUNDS TO FIGHT ZIKA.

    In addition to the purposes otherwise provided for Ebola funds made 
available to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention under title 
VII of division G of Public Law 113-235, such funds may also be used 
for Zika virus response and preparedness.
