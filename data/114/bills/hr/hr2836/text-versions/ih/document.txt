                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 to expand the number of 
employers required to provide a reasonable time and place for employees 
                   to express milk at the workplace.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. THIS ACT MAY BE CITED AS THE ``FAIR ACCESS FOR 
              MOMS ACT''.

SEC. 2. EMPLOYERS REQUIRED TO PROVIDE REASONABLE TIME AND PLACE FOR 
              EXPRESSING MILK.

    Section 7(r)(3) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 (29 U.S.C. 
207(r)(3)) is amended by striking ``50''and inserting ``15''.
