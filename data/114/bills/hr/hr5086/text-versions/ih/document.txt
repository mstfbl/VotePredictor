                                 A BILL


 
  To more accurately identify and transfer subsurface gravel sources 
   originally intended to be made available to the Ukpeagvik Inupiat 
  Corporation in exchange for its relinquishment of related property 
                                rights.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TECHNICAL AMENDMENT.

    Section 3 of the Barrow Gas Field Transfer Act of 1984 (Public Law 
98-366, 98 Stat. 468) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
``The foregoing conveyance to UIC shall include all right, title, and 
interest held by the United States to sand and gravel deposits 
underlying the surface estate owned by UIC contiguous to the Barrow gas 
fields, more particularly described as the sand and gravel deposits 
underlying that UIC surface estate lying within T 21N R16W, sections 7, 
17-18, 19-21, and 28-29; T 21N R17W, sections 1-2, and 11-14; T 22N, 
R18W, sections 4, 9, and 29-32; and T 22N R19W, sections 25 and 36.''.
