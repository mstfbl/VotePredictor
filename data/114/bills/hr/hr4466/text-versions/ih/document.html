                                 A BILL


 
To exempt the Lower Bois d'Arc Creek Reservoir Project from the Federal 
                      Water Pollution Control Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``North Texas Reservoir Approval 
Act''.

SEC. 2. LOWER BOIS D'ARC CREEK RESERVOIR PROJECT, FANNIN COUNTY, TEXAS.

    The Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1251 et seq.) 
shall not apply with respect to the construction of, impoundment of 
water in, and operation of, the Lower Bois d'Arc Creek Reservoir 
Project, including any associated water transmission facilities, by the 
North Texas Municipal Water District in Fannin County, Texas.
