                                 A BILL


 
    To direct the Commissioner of Food and Drugs to issue an order 
                withdrawing approval for Essure System.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``E-Free Act''.

SEC. 2. WITHDRAWAL OF PREMARKET APPROVAL FOR ESSURE SYSTEM.

    Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the 
Commissioner of Food and Drugs shall issue an order under section 
515(e) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. 360e(e)) 
withdrawing approval for Essure System.
