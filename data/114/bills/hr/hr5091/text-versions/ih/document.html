                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to reinstate the requirement for 
an annual report on the capacity of the Department of Veterans Affairs 
   to provide for specialized treatment and rehabilitative needs of 
                           disabled veterans.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Appropriate Care for Disabled 
Veterans Act''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF REQUIREMENT FOR REPORT ON THE CAPACITY OF THE 
              DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS TO PROVIDE FOR SPECIALIZED 
              TREATMENT AND REHABILITATIVE NEEDS OF DISABLED VETERANS.

    The first sentence of section 1706(b)(5)(A) of title 38, United 
States Code, is amended by striking ``through 2008''.
