                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 to require 
   employers to provide menstrual hygiene products for employees in 
                          workplace bathrooms.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Menstrual Products for Employees Act 
of 2016''.

SEC. 2. MENSTRUAL HYGIENE PRODUCTS.

    Section 6 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (29 
U.S.C. 655) is amended by adding at the end the following:
    ``(h) The Secretary shall by rule promulgate a requirement that 
each employer provide menstrual hygiene products free of charge for 
employees in the bathrooms of the employer.''.
