                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act of 2014 to 
      designate 20 graduate medical education residency positions 
                specifically for the study of optometry.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. GRADUATE MEDICAL EDUCATION RESIDENCY POSITIONS IN OPTOMETRY.

    Section 301(b)(2)(A) of the Veterans Access, Choice, and 
Accountability Act of 2014 (Public Law 113-146; 38 U.S.C. 7302 note) is 
amended by striking ``positions.'' and inserting ``positions, including 
up to 20 positions specifically designated for residencies in 
optometry.''.
