                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit any appropriation of funds to the Saint Lawrence Seaway 
            Development Corporation, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FUNDING PROHIBITION.

    No funds are authorized to be appropriated for the programs, 
activities, or expenses of the Saint Lawrence Seaway Development 
Corporation, as created by section 1 of the Act of May 13, 1954 (68 
Stat. 93, chapter 201; 33 U.S.C. 981).
