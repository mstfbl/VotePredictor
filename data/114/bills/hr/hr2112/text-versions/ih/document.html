                                 A BILL


 
   To direct the United States Postal Service to designate a single, 
           unique ZIP Code for North Plainfield, New Jersey.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SINGLE, UNIQUE ZIP CODE FOR NORTH PLAINFIELD, NEW JERSEY.

    Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this 
section, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, 
unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing only North 
Plainfield, New Jersey.
