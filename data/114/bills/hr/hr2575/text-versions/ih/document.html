                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007 to postpone a scheduled 
       increase in the minimum wage applicable to American Samoa.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. MINIMUM WAGE APPLICABLE TO AMERICAN SAMOA.

    Section 8103(b)(2)(C) of the Fair Minimum Wage Act of 2007 (29 
U.S.C. 206(b)(2)(C) note) is amended by striking ``and 2014'' and 
inserting ``2014, 2015, and 2016''.
