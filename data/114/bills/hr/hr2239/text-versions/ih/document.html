                                 A BILL


 
  To amend the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 to increase the target 
            financing of exports by small business concerns.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FINANCING TARGETS OF SMALL BUSINESS CONCERNS.

    Section 2(b)(1)(E)(v) of the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 (12 
U.S.C. 635(b)(1)(E)(v)) is amended by striking ``20 percent'' and 
inserting ``25 percent''.
