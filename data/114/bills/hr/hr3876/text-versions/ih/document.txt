                                 A BILL


 
     To protect consumer privacy during the development and use of 
                    autonomous vehicle technologies.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Autonomous Vehicle Privacy 
Protection Act of 2015''.

SEC. 2. GAO REPORT.

    The Comptroller General of the United States shall make available 
to the public a report that assesses the organizational readiness of 
the Department of Transportation to address autonomous vehicle 
technology challenges, including consumer privacy protections.
