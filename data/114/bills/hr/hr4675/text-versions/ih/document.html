                                 A BILL


 
 To direct the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration to 
  prohibit the use of leaded fuel by aircraft operating within United 
                            States airspace.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``No Lead in the Air Act of 2016''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON LEADED FUEL.

    The Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration, in 
consultation with the Administrator of the Environmental Protection 
Agency, shall issue such regulations as are necessary to prohibit the 
use of leaded fuel by aircraft operating within United States airspace 
beginning on January 1, 2021.
