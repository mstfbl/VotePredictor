                                 A BILL


 
To prohibit the National Science Foundation from obligating amounts for 
     the Polar Learning and Responding Climate Change Educational 
                              Partnership.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FUNDING PROHIBITION.

    The National Science Foundation may not obligate any amounts on or 
after the date of enactment of this Act for the Polar Learning and 
Responding Climate Change Educational Partnership.
