                                 A BILL


 
   To amend title 23, United States Code, to include bridges on the 
                 National Highway Performance Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Safe Bridges Investment Act of 
2015''.

SEC. 2. ELIGIBLE PROJECTS.

    Section 119(d)(2) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by 
adding at the end the following:
                    ``(Q) Construction, reconstruction, resurfacing, 
                restoration, rehabilitation, and preservation of, or 
                operational improvements for, a bridge not on the 
                National Highway System if such bridge is on the 
                Federal-aid Highway System''.
