                                 A BILL


 
     To prohibit land border crossing fees, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Promoting Border Commerce and Travel 
Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON LAND BORDER CROSSING FEES.

    The Secretary of Homeland Security may not establish, collect, or 
otherwise impose a border crossing fee for pedestrians or passenger 
vehicles at land ports of entry along the southern border or the 
northern border of the United States, and may not conduct any study 
relating to the imposition of such a fee.
