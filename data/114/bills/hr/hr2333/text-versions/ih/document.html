                                 A BILL


 
To authorize the Secretary of the Interior to acquire certain property 
related to the Fort Scott National Historic Site in Fort Scott, Kansas.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION TO ACQUIRE LUNETTE BLAIR.

    The first section of the Act entitled ``An Act to authorize 
establishment of the Fort Scott National Historic Site, Kansas, and for 
other purposes.'', approved October 19, 1978 (Public Law 95-484) is 
amended by striking ``: Provided, that the buildings so acquired shall 
not include the structure known as `Lunette Blair'''.
