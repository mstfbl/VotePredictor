                                 A BILL


 
To express the sense of Congress with respect to enabling Food and Drug 
                 Administration scientific engagement.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ENABLING FDA SCIENTIFIC ENGAGEMENT.

    It is the sense of Congress that participation in or sponsorship of 
scientific conferences and meetings is essential to the mission of the 
Food and Drug Administration.
