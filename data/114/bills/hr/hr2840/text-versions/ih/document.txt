                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit any appropriation of funds for the Science and Technology 
            account of the Environmental Protection Agency.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. FUNDING PROHIBITION.

    No funds are authorized to be appropriated for the Science and 
Technology account of the Environmental Protection Agency.
