                                 A BILL


 
  To amend chapter 44 of title 18, United States Code, to extend the 
   period during which a firearms licensee is required to wait for a 
  response from the national instant criminal background check system 
before being allowed to transfer a firearm to a prospective transferee.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF THE FIREARMS TRANSFER WAITING PERIOD.

    Section 922(t)(1)(B)(ii) of title 18 United States Code, is amended 
by striking ``3'' and inserting ``14''.

SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    The amendment made by this Act shall take effect 90 days after the 
date of the enactment of this Act.
