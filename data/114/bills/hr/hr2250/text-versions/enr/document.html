                                 An Act


 
               Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2016

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the Continuing 
Appropriations Act, 2016 (Public Law 114-53) is amended by striking the 
date specified in section 106(3) and inserting ``December 16, 2015''.
     This Act may be cited as the ``Further Continuing Appropriations 
Act, 2016''.

                               Speaker of the House of Representatives.
