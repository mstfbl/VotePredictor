                                 A BILL


 
   To revise repayment terms for certain loans made under the Lowell 
      National Historical Park Historic Preservation Loan Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Lowell National Historical Park 
Creative Partnerships Act''.

SEC. 2. REAUTHORIZATION.

    Section 303(a)(1) of the Act entitled ``An act to provide for the 
establishment of the Lowell National Historical Park in the 
Commonwealth of Massachusetts, and for other purposes'', approved June 
5, 1978 (Public Law 95-290; 16 U.S.C. 410cc et seq.), is amended by 
inserting after ``thirty-five years'' the following: ``, except that 
any such loan made in 1984 shall have a maturity of forty-two years''.
