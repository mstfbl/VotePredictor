                                 An Act


 
    To designate the Department of Veterans Affairs community-based 
   outpatient clinic in Laughlin, Nevada, as the ``Master Chief Petty 
                     Officer Jesse Dean VA Clinic''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICER JESSE DEAN VA CLINIC.
    (a) Designation.--The Department of Veterans Affairs community-
based outpatient clinic in Laughlin, Nevada, shall after the date of 
the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the ``Master Chief 
Petty Officer Jesse Dean VA Clinic''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the community-based 
outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a 
reference to the ``Master Chief Petty Officer Jesse Dean VA Clinic''.
