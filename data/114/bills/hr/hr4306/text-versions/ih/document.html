                                 A BILL


 
     To make permanent the Advisory Committee on Minority Veterans.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Advisory Committee on Minority 
Veterans Act of 2015''.

SEC. 2. ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON MINORITY VETERANS MADE PERMANENT.

    Section 544 of title 38 of the United States Code is amended by 
striking ``(e) The Committee shall cease to exist December 31, 2016.''.
