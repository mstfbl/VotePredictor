                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
   at 836 Anacapa Street, Santa Barbara, California as the ``Special 
   Warfare Operator Master Chief Petty Officer (SEAL) Louis `Lou' J. 
                    Langlais Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SPECIAL WARFARE OPERATOR MASTER CHIEF PETTY OFFICER (SEAL) 
              LOUIS ``LOU'' J. LANGLAIS POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 836 Anacapa Street, Santa Barbara, California, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Special Warfare Operator Master Chief 
Petty Officer (SEAL) Louis `Lou' J. Langlais Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Special 
Warfare Operator Master Chief Petty Officer (SEAL) Louis `Lou' J. 
Langlais Post Office Building''.
