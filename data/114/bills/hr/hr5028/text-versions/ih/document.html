                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
 at 10721 E Jefferson Ave in Detroit, Michigan, as the ``Mary Eleanora 
                     McCoy Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. MARY ELEANORA MCCOY POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 10721 E Jefferson Ave in Detroit, Michigan, shall be known 
and designated as the ``Mary Eleanora McCoy Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Mary 
Eleanora McCoy Post Office Building''.
