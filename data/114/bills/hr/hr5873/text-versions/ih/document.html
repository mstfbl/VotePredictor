                                 A BILL


 
To designate the Federal building and United States courthouse located 
    at 511 East San Antonio Avenue in El Paso, Texas, as the ``R.E. 
       Thomason Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 511 
East San Antonio Avenue in El Paso, Texas, shall be known and 
designated as the ``R.E. Thomason Federal Building and United States 
Courthouse''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the Federal building and United States 
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference 
to the ``R.E. Thomason Federal Building and United States Courthouse''.
