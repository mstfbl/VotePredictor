                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 5, United States Code, to exempt the Internal Revenue 
     Service from certain labor-management relations requirements.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``End the Partisan IRS Culture Act'' 
or the ``EPIC Act''.

SEC. 2. DEFINITIONS; APPLICATION.

    Section 7103(a)(3) of title 5, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subparagraph (G), by striking ``or'' at the end;
            (2) in subparagraph (H), by striking the period at the end 
        and inserting ``; or''; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following:
                    ``(I) the Internal Revenue Service.''.
