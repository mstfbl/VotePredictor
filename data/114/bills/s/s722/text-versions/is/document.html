                                 A BILL


 
 To extend the date after which interest earned on obligations held in 
   the wildlife restoration fund may be available for apportionment.

United States of America in Congress assembled,

              FUND.

    Section 3(b)(2)(C) of the Pittman-Robertson Wildlife Restoration 
Act (16 U.S.C. 669b(b)(2)(C)) is amended by striking ``2016'' and 
inserting ``2026''.
