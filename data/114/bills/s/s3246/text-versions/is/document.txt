                                 A BILL


 
    To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend authority for 
  operation of the Department of Veterans Affairs Regional Office in 
                Manila, the Republic of the Philippines.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY FOR OPERATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF 
              VETERANS AFFAIRS REGIONAL OFFICE IN MANILA, THE REPUBLIC 
              OF THE PHILIPPINES.

    Section 315(b) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by 
striking ``September 30, 2016'' and inserting ``September 30, 2017''.
