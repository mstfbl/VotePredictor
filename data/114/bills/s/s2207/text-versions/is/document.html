                                 A BILL


 
  To require the Secretary of State to offer rewards of not less than 
 $10,000,000 for information that leads to the arrest or conviction of 
     suspects in connection with the bombing of Pan Am Flight 103.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REWARDS AUTHORIZED.

    In accordance with the Rewards for Justice program authorized under 
section 36(b) of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 (22 
U.S.C. 2708(b)), the Secretary of State shall offer a reward of not 
less than $10,000,000 to any individual who furnishes information 
leading to the arrest or conviction in any country of any individual 
responsible for the 1988 bombing of Pan Am Flight 103.
