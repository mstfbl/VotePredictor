                                 A BILL


 
    To repeal the jury duty exemption for elected officials of the 
                          legislative branch.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Civic Duty Act''.

SEC. 2. REPEAL OF JURY DUTY EXEMPTION FOR LEGISLATIVE BRANCH OFFICIALS.

    Section 310 of the Legislative Branch Appropriations Act, 1991 (2 
U.S.C. 30a) is repealed.
