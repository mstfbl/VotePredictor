                                 A BILL


 
To provide that section 4108(5)(C)(iv) of the Elementary and Secondary 
         Education Act of 1965 may be known as ``Bree's Law''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. BREE'S LAW.

    Clause (iv) of section 4108(5)(C) of the Elementary and Secondary 
Education Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 7118(5)(C)(iv)), as amended by section 
4101 of the Every Student Succeeds Act (Public Law 114-95; 129 Stat. 
1968), may be cited as ``Bree's Law'', and programs or activities 
carried out under such clause may be referred to as ``Bree's Law 
programs'' or ``Bree's Law activities'', respectively.
