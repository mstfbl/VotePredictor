                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Delta Development Act to include Vernon and Sabine 
     parishes in the definition of the term ``Lower Mississippi''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. VERNON AND SABINE PARISHES.

    Section 4(2)(D) of the Delta Development Act (42 U.S.C. 3121 note; 
Public Law 100-460) is amended by inserting ``Vernon, Sabine,'' after 
``Webster''.
