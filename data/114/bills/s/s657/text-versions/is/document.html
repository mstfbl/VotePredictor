                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend to all veterans with a 
  serious service-connected injury eligibility to participate in the 
                   family caregiver services program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Caregivers Expansion and Improvement 
Act of 2015''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION TO ALL VETERANS WITH A SERIOUS SERVICE-CONNECTED 
              DISABILITY OF ELIGIBILITY FOR PARTICIPATION IN FAMILY 
              CAREGIVER PROGRAM.

    Section 1720G(a)(2)(B) of title 38, United States Code, is amended 
by striking ``on or after September 11, 2001''.
