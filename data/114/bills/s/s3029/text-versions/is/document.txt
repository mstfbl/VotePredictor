                                 A BILL


 
  To extend the authorization of appropriations to the Department of 
 Veterans Affairs for purposes of awarding grants to veterans service 
     organizations for the transportation of highly rural veterans.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Maurice (Mo) Bailey Highly Rural 
Veteran Transportation Grant Reauthorization Act of 2016''.

SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS FOR GRANTS TO 
              VETERANS SERVICE ORGANIZATIONS FOR TRANSPORTATION OF 
              HIGHLY RURAL VETERANS.

    Section 307(d) of the Caregivers and Veterans Omnibus Health 
Services Act of 2010 (Public Law 111-163; 124 Stat. 1154; 38 U.S.C. 
1710 note) is amended by striking ``2016'' and inserting ``2017''.
