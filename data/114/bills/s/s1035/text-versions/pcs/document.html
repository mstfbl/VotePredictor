                                 A BILL


 
To extend authority relating to roving surveillance, access to business 
 records, and individual terrorists as agents of foreign powers under 
    the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSIONS OF AUTHORITY UNDER THE FOREIGN INTELLIGENCE 
              SURVEILLANCE ACT OF 1978.

    (a) Roving Surveillance and Access to Business Records.--Section 
102(b)(1) of the USA PATRIOT Improvement and Reauthorization Act of 
2005 (50 U.S.C. 1805 note) is amended by striking ``June 1, 2015'' and 
inserting ``December 31, 2020''.
    (b) Individual Terrorists as Agents of Foreign Powers.--Section 
6001(b)(1) of the Intelligence Reform and Terrorism Prevention Act of 
2004 (50 U.S.C. 1801 note) is amended by striking ``June 1, 2015'' and 
inserting ``December 31, 2020''.
