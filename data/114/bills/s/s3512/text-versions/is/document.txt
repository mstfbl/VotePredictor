                                 A BILL


 
    To reauthorize the Historically Black Colleges and Universities 
                     Historic Preservation Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. HISTORICALLY BLACK COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES HISTORIC 
              PRESERVATION PROGRAM REAUTHORIZED.

    Section 507(d)(2) of division I of the Omnibus Parks and Public 
Lands Management Act of 1996 (54 U.S.C. 302101 note; Public Law 104-
333) is amended by striking the period at the end and inserting ``and 
each of fiscal years 2017 through 2023.''.
