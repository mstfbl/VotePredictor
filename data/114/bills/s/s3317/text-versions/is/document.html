                                 A BILL


 
    To prohibit the further extension or establishment of national 
   monuments in the State of Utah except by express authorization of 
                               Congress.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. LIMITATION ON FURTHER EXTENSION OR ESTABLISHMENT OF NATIONAL 
              MONUMENTS IN UTAH.

    Section 320301(d) of title 54, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in the subsection heading, by inserting ``or Utah'' 
        after ``Wyoming''; and
            (2) by inserting ``or Utah'' after ``Wyoming''.
