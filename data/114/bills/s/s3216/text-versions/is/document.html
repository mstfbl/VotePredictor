                                 A BILL


 
To repeal the Act entitled ``An Act to confer jurisdiction on the State 
 of Iowa over offenses committed by or against Indians on the Sac and 
                       Fox Indian Reservation''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL.

    The Act entitled ``An Act to confer jurisdiction on the State of 
Iowa over offenses committed by or against Indians on the Sac and Fox 
Indian Reservation'', approved June 30, 1948 (62 Stat. 1161, chapter 
759), is repealed.
