                                 A BILL


 
To provide for the withdrawal of certain Federal land in Garden Valley, 
                                Nevada.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Garden Valley Withdrawal Act''.

SEC. 2. GARDEN VALLEY, NEVADA, WITHDRAWAL.

    Subject to valid existing rights in existence on the date of 
enactment of this Act, the approximately 805,100 acres of Federal land 
generally depicted on the map entitled ``Garden Valley Withdrawal 
Area'' and dated July 11, 2014, is withdrawn from--
            (1) entry, appropriation, and disposal under the public 
        land laws;
            (2) location, entry, and patent under the mining laws; and
            (3) operation of the mineral leasing, mineral materials, 
        and geothermal leasing laws.
