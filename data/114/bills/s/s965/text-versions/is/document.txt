                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the use of funds by Internal Revenue Service to target 
citizens of the United States for exercising any right guaranteed under 
     the First Amendment to the Constitution of the United States.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITION ON TARGETING BY THE INTERNAL REVENUE SERVICE 
              BASED ON THE EXERCISE OF FIRST AMENDMENT RIGHTS.

    None of the funds made available under any Act may be used by the 
Internal Revenue Service to target citizens of the United States for 
exercising any right guaranteed under the First Amendment to the 
Constitution of the United States.
