                          Concurrent Resolution

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF EMANCIPATION HALL FOR CEREMONY TO PRESENT 
              CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL TO AMERICAN FIGHTER ACES.

    Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be 
used on May 20, 2015, for a ceremony to present the Congressional Gold 
Medal to the American Fighter Aces collectively, in recognition of 
their heroic military service and defense of our country's freedom 
throughout the history of aviation warfare. Physical preparations for 
the conduct of the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with 
such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
