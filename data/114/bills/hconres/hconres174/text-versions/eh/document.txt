                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

strengthen the tsunami detection, forecast, warning, research, and 
mitigation program of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric 
Administration, and for other purposes, the Clerk of the House of 
Representatives shall make the following correction: Amend the

development, and delivery of 21st century cures, and for other 
purposes.''.
