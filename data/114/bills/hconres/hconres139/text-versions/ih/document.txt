                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION


 
       Providing for a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 2577.

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring), 
That in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 2577, the Clerk of the House of 
Representatives shall amend the long title so as to read: ``Making 
appropriations for military construction, the Department of Veterans 
Affairs, and related agencies for the fiscal year ending September 30, 
2017, and for other purposes.''.
