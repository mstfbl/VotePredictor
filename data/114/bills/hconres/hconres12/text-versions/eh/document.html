                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF ROTUNDA OF THE CAPITOL FOR CEREMONY TO PRESENT 
              CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL TO JACK NICKLAUS.

    The rotunda of the United States Capitol is authorized to be used 
on March 24, 2015, for a ceremony to present the Congressional Gold 
Medal to Jack Nicklaus.
 Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony shall be carried 
out in accordance with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol 
may prescribe.

            Passed the House of Representatives February 2, 2015.
