                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF EMANCIPATION HALL FOR CEREMONY TO COMMEMORATE 150TH 
              ANNIVERSARY OF RATIFICATION OF 13TH AMENDMENT.

    (a) Authorization.--Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center 
is authorized to be used on


              

 December 9, 2015, for a ceremony to commemorate the 150th anniversary 
of the ratification of the 13th Amendment to the Constitution of the 
United States, which abolished slavery in the United States.
    (b) Preparations.-- Physical preparations for the conduct of the 
ceremony described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance 
with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.

            Passed the House of Representatives November 16, 2015.
