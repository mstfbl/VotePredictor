        H.J.Res.76

                     One Hundred Fourteenth Congress

                                 of the

                        United States of America


                          AT THE FIRST SESSION

          Begun and held at the City of Washington on Tuesday,
           the sixth day of January, two thousand and fifteen


                            Joint Resolution


 
 Appointing the day for the convening of the second session of the One 
                      Hundred Fourteenth Congress.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That the second regular 
session of the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress shall begin at noon on 
Monday, January 4, 2016.

                               Speaker of the House of Representatives.

                            Vice President of the United States and    
                                               President of the Senate.
