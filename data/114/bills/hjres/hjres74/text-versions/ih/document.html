                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
   United States Code, of the final rule of the Administrator of the 
  Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``National Ambient Air 
                     Quality Standards for Ozone''.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Administrator of the Environmental Protection 
Agency relating to ``National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone'' 
(80 Fed. Reg. 65292 (October 26, 2015)), and such rule shall have no 
force or effect.
