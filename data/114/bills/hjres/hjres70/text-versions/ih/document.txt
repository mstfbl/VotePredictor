                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
 Disapproving a rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency 
   relating to ``National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone''.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to 
``National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone'' (contained in the 
docket numbered EPA-HQ-OAR-2008-0699), and such rule shall have no 
force or effect.
