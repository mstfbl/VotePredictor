H. Res. 292

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                         June 10, 2015.
    Resolved, That on such date as the Speaker of the House of Representatives 
may designate, official photographs of the House may be taken while the House is 
in actual session. Payment for the costs associated with taking, preparing, and 
distributing such photographs may be made from the applicable accounts of the 
House of Representatives.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
