H. Res. 8

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                       January 6, 2015.
    Resolved, That pursuant to the Legislative Pay Act of 1929, as amended, the 
six minority employees authorized therein shall be the following named persons, 
effective January 6, 2015, until otherwise ordered by the House, to-wit: Nadeam 
Elshami, George Kundanis, Diane Dewhirst, Richard Meltzer, Wyndee Parker, and 
Drew Hammill, each to receive gross compensation pursuant to the provisions of 
House Resolution 119, Ninety-fifth Congress, as enacted into permanent law by 
section 115 of Public Law 95-94. In addition, the Minority Leader may appoint 
and set the annual rate of pay for up to 3 further minority employees.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
