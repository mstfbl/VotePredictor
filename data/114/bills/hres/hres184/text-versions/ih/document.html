                               RESOLUTION


 
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to require the House 
             to meet 5 days a week for 39 weeks each year.

    Resolved, That (a) rule XIV of the Rules of the House of 
Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new 
clause:
    ``7. There shall be votes held in the House or in the Committee of 
the Whole House on the State of the Union for 5 consecutive days each 
week except--
            ``(a) during any recess there shall be no votes, except 
        there shall be not more than 13 recess weeks during each 
        calendar year; or
            ``(b) during any week in which there is a holiday, then 
        votes shall be held on 4 days.''.
    (b) The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect with the 
first session of Congress beginning on the date of adoption of this 
resolution.
