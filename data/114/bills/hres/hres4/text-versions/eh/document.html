H. Res. 4

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                       January 6, 2015.
    Resolved, That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United 
States that the House of Representatives has elected John A. Boehner, a 
Representative from the State of Ohio as Speaker, and Karen L. Haas, a citizen 
of the State of Maryland as Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One 
Hundred Fourteenth Congress.
