H. Res. 233

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                         June 15, 2015.
Whereas three United States citizens have been held captive in Iran, some of 
        them for multiple years;
Whereas one United States citizen disappeared in Iran over 8 years ago; and
Whereas Iranian President Hassan Rouhani has stated that his government wishes 
        to engage in a constructive interaction with the world: Now, therefore, 
        be it
    Resolved, That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that Iran 
should release all detained United States citizens immediately and provide any 
information it possesses regarding any United States citizens that have 
disappeared within its borders.
