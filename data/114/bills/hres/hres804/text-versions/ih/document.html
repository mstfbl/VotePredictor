                               RESOLUTION


 
      Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to allow 
independent, non-government television cameras to broadcast House floor 
                              proceedings.

    Resolved, That clause 2(b) of rule V of the Rules of the House of 
Representatives is amended by inserting before the period at the end 
thereof the following: ``and shall be permitted to record and broadcast 
at any time a Member is present on the floor.''.
