                               RESOLUTION


 
    Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives regarding 
                          hydroelectric power.

    Resolved, That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that 
hydroelectric power is the most abundant source of clean, renewable 
energy in the United States and should be fully utilized in the 
Nation's pursuit of energy independence and affordable energy for all 
people of the United States.
