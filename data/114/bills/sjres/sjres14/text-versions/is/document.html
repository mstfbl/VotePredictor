                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
  Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, 
United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Federal Communications 
            Commission regulating broadband Internet access.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to 
regulating broadband Internet access (published at 80 Fed. Reg. 19737 
(April 13, 2015)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
