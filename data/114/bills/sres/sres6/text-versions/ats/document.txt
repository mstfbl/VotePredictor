                               RESOLUTION


 
 Expressing the thanks of the Senate to the Honorable Patrick J. Leahy 
 for his service as President Pro Tempore of the United States Senate 
and to designate Senator Leahy as President Pro Tempore Emeritus of the 
                         United States Senate.

    Resolved, That the United States Senate expresses its deepest 
gratitude to Senator Patrick J. Leahy for his dedication and commitment 
during his service to the Senate as the President Pro Tempore.
    Further, as a token of appreciation of the Senate for his long and 
faithful service, Senator Patrick J. Leahy is hereby designated 
President Pro Tempore Emeritus of the United States Senate.
