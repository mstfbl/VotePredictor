                               RESOLUTION


 
Amending rule XXVIII of the Standing Rules of the Senate to provide for 
             timely establishment of conference committees.

    Resolved,

SECTION 1. MOTIONS TO GO TO CONFERENCE.

    Rule XXVIII of the Standing Rules of the Senate is amended by 
striking paragraph 2(b) and inserting the following:
    ``(b) Consideration of a motion described in subparagraph (a), 
including consideration of any debatable motion or appeal in connection 
therewith, shall be limited to not more than 2 hours.''.
