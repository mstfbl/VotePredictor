                               RESOLUTION


 
Providing for members on the part of the Senate of the Joint Committee 
    on Printing and the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library.

elected members of the following joint committees of Congress:
    Joint Committee on Printing: Mr. Blunt, Mr. Roberts, Mr. Boozman, 
Mr. Schumer, and Mr. Udall.
    Joint Committee of Congress on the Library: Mr. Blunt, Mr. Roberts, 
Mrs. Capito, Mr. Schumer, and Mr. Leahy.
